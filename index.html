I started writing my book when I was about 11 years old. It was dense, a story about a girl who used to live alone and now had to adjust to living with siblings. It was my story, and I just didn't know how else to tell it. After that, the story evolved a lot of times, until what it is now. A thriller about a nerd and an artist trying to uncover world-shattering secrets.
